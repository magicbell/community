---
title: Email
---

By default, MagicBell delivers email notifications to your users using our email infrastructure. These notifications come from `notifications@magicbell-notifications.com`.

MagicBell can be configured to deliver email notifications for your users using third party email providers such as [SendGrid](/docs/email/sendgrid), [Postmark](/docs/email/postmark), [Mailgun](/docs/email/mailgun), and [Amazon SES](/docs/email/amazon-ses).
